# Delivery Checks

Before handing off, confirm the work meets both visual and engineering expectations.

## Visual checks

- The interface has a clearly identifiable direction.
- Typography, spacing, color, and surface treatment feel cohesive.
- The result avoids obvious template-like or generic AI aesthetics.
- The strongest visual idea is noticeable without overwhelming the content.

## Product checks

- The implementation still fits the product's audience and purpose.
- Existing design-system boundaries were respected when applicable.
- The requested surface is meaningfully improved, not merely restyled at random.

## Engineering checks

- The code is real, runnable, and consistent with the existing stack.
- The change does not introduce unjustified dependencies or fragile styling hacks.
- Responsive behavior is accounted for on both desktop and mobile.
- Reduced motion, focus visibility, and readable contrast were considered.

## Scope checks

- The diff size matches the request.
- Unrelated surfaces were not restyled.
- Any assumptions about tone, audience, or redesign level are stated briefly if they affected the result.
